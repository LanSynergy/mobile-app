Music from your server.
Polished like the apps you wish existed.

Aetherfin streams from your self-hosted Jellyfin and decodes everything on-device with libmpv. FLAC, ALAC, OPUS, WAV — bit-for-bit. No transcoding, no telemetry, no cloud.

Android 7.0+ · Requires a Jellyfin 10.8+ server

Built for the way you actually listen

Every detail tuned for a premium music experience, not a "Jellyfin client".

Real-time FFT visualizer

64-band spectrum from libmpv's post-DSP output, rendered at vsync with path-batched painting. No RECORD_AUDIO permission needed.

Gapless playback

libmpv pre-fetches the next track in the background. Album-side transitions you didn't know you missed until you hear them again.

Equalizer & DSP

Bass, treble, EBU R128 loudness normalization, dynamic compressor, ReplayGain. All running through ffmpeg's filter pipeline natively.

Synced lyrics

LRC parsed on-device, auto-scrolling line highlight, mirrored to your lock-screen via Android's METADATA_KEY_LYRICS.

Sleep timer

5/10/15/30/45/60 minute presets, plus an "end of track" mode that pauses cleanly when the current song finishes.

Privacy first

No analytics. No crash reporters. No third-party trackers. Credentials live in Android's secure storage. Your server, your data.

mDNS discovery

Aetherfin auto-discovers Jellyfin servers on your local network. Or punch in a URL — VPN and public HTTPS both work.

Bit-perfect output

Force a sample rate, bit depth, exclusive mode, or S/PDIF passthrough. For the audiophile DAC nerds — you know who you are.

A glimpse

Spectral colors extracted from your cover art. Premium typography. Quiet motion.

Up and running in three steps

  1. 1

    Download the APK

    Grab app-release.apk from the latest release. Per-ABI builds are smaller; the universal one works everywhere.

    Latest release →
  2. 2

    Connect your Jellyfin server

    mDNS will list servers on your local network. Or paste a URL — LAN, VPN, or public HTTPS all work.

  3. 3

    Sign in and play

    Aetherfin treats Jellyfin as a passive file source. Tracks stream as raw bytes via /Audio/{id}/stream?Static=true — no HLS transcoding round-trip.
